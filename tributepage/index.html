How Alex Pereira went from a drunk to the face of the UFC.

Timeline of Alex Pereira's Road to Championship

  • 2011: Alex Pereira was struggling with alcoholism and working as a bouncer in a bar.
  • 2012: Pereira began training in mixed martial arts (MMA) seriously, transitioning from a career in kickboxing.
  • 2013: Pereira made his professional MMA debut and started gaining recognition in the Brazilian MMA scene.
  • 2015: Pereira won the Jungle Fight Middleweight Championship, a significant achievement in his early career.
  • 2017: Pereira captured the Glory Middleweight Championship, marking his success in the kickboxing world.
  • 2020: Pereira made his UFC debut, entering the promotion with a reputation as a striking powerhouse.
  • 2021: Pereira won the UFC Middleweight Championship, defeating Israel Adesanya.
  • 2023: Alex Pereira became the UFC Light Heavyweight Champion after a successful fight at the top of the division.
