About the Husqvarna 501FE

The Husqvarna 501FE is a high-performance off-road motorcycle designed for enthusiasts who demand the best. It features a powerful engine, advanced suspension, and a lightweight frame for superior handling and comfort. With its robust build, the 501FE is perfect for tackling tough terrains and long rides. Its modern design includes ergonomic controls and advanced technology for an enhanced riding experience.

Key Features

The 501FE comes equipped with state-of-the-art features, including a fuel-injected 4-stroke engine, a fully adjustable suspension system, and a durable aluminum frame. The bike is also designed with rider comfort in mind, featuring an adjustable seat and advanced electronic controls. Whether you're a seasoned rider or a newcomer, the 501FE offers exceptional performance and reliability.
